Swim Two Birds is in the Jackson Heights Historic District at 76-05 37th Avenue, between 76th and 77th Streets.

Subway

The 7, E, F, M & R trains stop three blocks from us at the Jackson Heights-Roosevelt Ave/Broadway-74th St Station.

Parking

There is parking 1 1/2 blocks away at 37-18 75th Street. As of March 1, 2017, parking costs $4.50 for one hour/$8 for two. There is also parking on 37th Avenue and our side streets.

Nearby

We are about 2.5 miles from LaGuardia Airport to the north, and Citi Field and the National Tennis Center to the east, and 10-12 minutes by subway, plus a three block walk, from Midtown Manhattan (Lexington Av/53rd St) and the Upper East Side (Lexington Av/63rd St.)
