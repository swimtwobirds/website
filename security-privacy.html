For our restaurant, internet security and privacy come under three headings:

1. Browsing Our Website
When you visit our website, you do so over an encrypted connection. This is why our internet address starts with https://, and why you see a padlock or key symbol in your browser bar. If you click on the padlock or key, you will find information about our security certificate and encryption.

2. Making a Reservation
You can make a reservation by telephone or by filling out a form on our website. The form asks for your name, e-mail address and telephone number. ResDiary, the company that manages our online reservations, receives your details and provides them to us. Your reservation details are encrypted as they go from you to ResDiary and to us. ResDiary's website is at https://resdiary.com.

We keep records of our reservations in order to improve our retaurant and our service to customers.

3. Subscribing to Our Newsletter
We write a newsletter about our restaurant, specials and upcoming events. You can subscribe by filling out a form on our website. The form asks for your name and e-mail address. Campaign Monitor, the company that manages distribution of our newsletter, receives these details and provides them to us. Your name and e-mail address are encrypted as they go from you to Campaign Monitor and to us. Campaign Monitor's website is at https://www.campaignmonitor.com.

We do not share our list of subscribers with anyone. In addition, our newsletter contains a link to unsubscribe. If you choose to unsubscribe, you will no longer receive our newsletter, and your name and e-mail address will be deleted from our subscriber list.
